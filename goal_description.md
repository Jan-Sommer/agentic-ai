This file contains general information about our goals in regards of agentic AI.

## Goals
- lake the whole organization "agentic AI ready"
- offer clients the ability to point their agentic AI to our products
- let clients use our products via agentic AI
- still offer clients the possibility to use our services the "old way" via the UIs and APIs
- have a future proof architecture spanning both products, which allows to follow the agentic AI path
- be prepared to provide our own agentic AI, which clients may use to fulfill the use cases of our products
- being a one-stop go-to solution for supply chain and sustainability management

## Internal Goals
- make the products teams agentic AI ready, so they know how to work with the it
- setup security standards and guardrails, to ensure the usage of agentic AI is safe
- setup quality standards and guardrails, to ensure the results of the agentic AI fulfill our quality requirements
- foster knowledge share in the context of agentic AI
